### Key Insights on U.S. Listed Companies with Monopoly Positions in Six Domains

Research suggests that several U.S. listed companies hold dominant positions in AI, energy, defense, robotics, space, and cyber domains, often due to high market shares, regulatory barriers, or critical supply chain roles. Evidence leans toward companies like NVIDIA in AI GPUs and Lockheed Martin in defense primes as key players, though competition and policy shifts introduce some uncertainty. Small modular reactors (SMRs) in energy and quantum tech in cyber show emerging monopolies, but global supply chains add complexity.

#### Dominant Players by Domain
- **AI**: NVIDIA (over 80% GPU market share) and Vertiv (data center cooling leader) benefit from AI boom, with barriers like patents and scale.
- **Energy**: NuScale Power and BWX Technologies dominate SMRs; Constellation Energy leads nuclear operations.
- **Defense**: Lockheed Martin and RTX hold prime contractor advantages via ITAR certifications.
- **Robotics**: Intuitive Surgical (surgical robots) and Rockwell Automation (industrial automation) have strong moats.
- **Space**: Rocket Lab (small launches) and Iridium (satellite comms) fill niche monopolies.
- **Cyber**: Palo Alto Networks and CrowdStrike lead in security, with quantum firms like IonQ emerging.

#### Policy and Market Tailwinds
Companies benefiting from CHIPS Act, IRA, and NDAA include Intel, TSMC affiliates, and defense firms like RTX. Small caps under $10B, such as NuScale and Rocket Lab, offer growth potential but higher risk due to limited coverage.

#### Investment Considerations
Focus on a mix of large caps for stability (e.g., NVIDIA at $4.58T market cap) and small caps for upside (e.g., Oklo). Market shares above 30% and certifications like NRC create durable advantages, though geopolitical risks persist.

### AIドメインの詳細分析

AIインフラはGPU、データセンター冷却、電力供給、光学コンポーネント、ストレージ、サーバー組立などで構成され、NVIDIAがGPU市場で圧倒的シェアを維持している一方、冷却・電力分野ではVertivやEatonがボトルネックを形成。市場シェア30%超や規制障壁が少ないため競争激化中だが、AIブームによる需要急増で成長余地大。

#### 主なポイント
- **NVIDIAの支配力**: GPU市場シェア80%以上でAIトレーニングのボトルネック。競合はAMDやIntelだが、CUDAエコシステムが差別化。
- **インフラ関連の隠れた勝者**: Vertiv（冷却）、Eaton（電力）、Equinix/Digital Realty（データセンター運営）がAIデータセンター拡大で恩恵。冷却市場は液冷移行が進み、Vertivがリーダー。
- **中小型株の成長性**: ModineやCelesticaのようなTier2サプライヤーが急伸中だが、ボラティリティ高。
- **政策追い風**: CHIPS Actが半導体関連を支援、IRAが電力インフラを後押し。
- **リスク**: 供給過剰や競争激化で株価変動大。2026年はデータセンター投資5300億ドル超の見込み。

#### 投資視点
大型株（NVIDIA、Broadcom）は安定、中小型株（Modine、Celestica）は高成長期待。市場全体でAIインフラ投資が急増中だが、地政学リスクやエネルギー制約に注意。
